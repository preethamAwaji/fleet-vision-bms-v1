INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADDING HARDWARE IMAGES
========================================

Please save your hardware images in this folder with the following names:

1. prototype_1.jpg
   - Your breadboard prototype image (first image you provided)
   - Shows complete setup with VSDSquadron, GPS, battery, breadboard
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080 or higher
   - Format: JPG or PNG

2. prototype_2.jpg
   - Your assembled BMS unit (second image you provided)
   - Shows compact assembly with organized wiring
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080 or higher
   - Format: JPG or PNG

3. schematic.png
   - Your circuit schematic image (from EasyEDA)
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080 or higher
   - Format: PNG or JPG

4. pcb_layout.png
   - Your PCB layout image (from EasyEDA)
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080 or higher
   - Format: PNG or JPG

5. BOM.csv (optional)
   - Bill of Materials in CSV format
   - Columns: Designator, Component, Value, Package, Quantity, Supplier, Part Number, Cost

6. gerber_files/ (optional)
   - Create a subfolder for Gerber manufacturing files
   - Include: .GTL, .GBL, .GTO, .GBO, .GTS, .GBS, .TXT (drill file)

After adding the images:
- They will automatically appear in the README.md
- The hardware/README.md will display them
- Users can view the complete hardware design
